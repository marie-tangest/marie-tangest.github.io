HIVA

My company, Hiva, is my own creation, giving me the freedom to select the perfect logo. I opted for something close to my heart – my adorable cat's face. Captivated by its charm, I stylised its features, making it unique. Black and yellow became the vibrant colors representing my brand. This logo isn't just about my beloved pet; it embodies the essence of my company!

NOOR

Noor is a digital agency dedicated to enhancing its clients' online growth.
  • The name “Noor” is derived from the Persian word for “light”. The client asked for a modern, friendly, and high-end logo that is eye-catching, and easy to remember. I was particularly drawn to the word "Noor," prompting me to incorporate symbolic elements into the logo's typography. This approach allows the company to utilize a singular symbol in limited media spaces, serving as both a practical solution and an effective attention-grabber.
  • The symbol was crafted by merging the two "o" characters. The infinity shape of the sign symbolises the connection between the business and its clientele, intertwining them in a journey towards mutual success.

VIU

Vän i Umeå is a nonprofit community association dedicated to assisting newcomers in making friends and integrating into society. Captivated by the concept, I began volunteering. Soon, I was invited to join the leadership team, where I initiated the design of their visual identity and introduced new communication materials, resulting in membership growth and increased recognition among citizens.
  • With a large existing group of members, the network wished to retain some elements of the old logo. Thus, I maintained the round shape while modernising the two figures. The figures, holding each other in a mutual embrace, symbolise equality and enjoyment in each other's company, with neither in a dominant position.
